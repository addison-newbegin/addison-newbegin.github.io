Recipe #1

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
Ground Beef Chuck 1 pound
Salt and Fresh Ground pepper a pinch
Butter As Needed

Step 1: Take beef out of the refrigerator and form the patties. Put a thumb print in the middle of the patty. Season with slt and fresh ground pepper.

Step 2: Melt 1 tablespoon of butter in a cast iron pan over medium heat. Cook hamburger patties 3 to 4 minutes on each side, depending on how well done you like it.

Step 3: Rest burgers covered loosely with aluminum foil for 5 minutes then serve on a buttered bun with lettuce, tomatoes and onion (or how you like it).

Recipe #3

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Sandwhich Bread 2 slices
Butter 2 teaspoons
american cheese 3 slices

Step 1:Preheat a cast-iron skillet or griddle over medium-low heat. Spread 1 teaspoon butter onto one side of each bread slice.

Step 2: Place 1 bread slice buttered side down in the skillet. Top with the cheese and the second bread slice buttered side up. Cook for 2 minutes. Flip carefully using a wide metal spatula, then cook for 2 minutes more on the second side; the bread should be barely colored. Repeat, cooking for about 2 minutes more on each side, until golden. Cut in half and serve immediately.
